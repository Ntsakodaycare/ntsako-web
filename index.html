About Us

At Ntsako DayCare, we believe every child deserves a foundation built on love, care, and opportunity. We provide high-quality early childhood education that sparks curiosity, creativity, and confidence in young learners.

Our children enjoy nutritious, balanced meals each day to support healthy growth and development. With our dedicated garden, they learn to connect with nature, experience the joy of planting, and develop healthy habits that last a lifetime.

The inspiration behind Ntsako DayCare began in 2000. From the very beginning, we were motivated to create a safe, nurturing environment where children can learn, play, and thrive. We continue to do our best to raise disciplined, confident, and well-rounded children.

Today, Ntsako DayCare is proud to be a place where families find trust, children discover joy in learning, and every child is prepared for a bright future.

We proudly follow the National Curriculum Framework (NCF) for all our early learning age groups, while our Grade R class is fully aligned with the CAPS curriculum. Our children enjoy fully resourced educational experiences that support exploration, learning through play, and age-appropriate development.

Our staff is fully trained and qualified to teach the ECD curriculum with professionalism, compassion, and dedication.
